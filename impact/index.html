The network partner

Co-operative platforms

  • Platforms that plug into the co-operative banks’ core systems
  • Sign up farmers and shops, and handle the paperwork
  • Connect village societies with district and state co-operative banks
  • Run the dashboards, help desk and staff training

The technology partner

NEXO

  • Turns applications into credit decisions, looking beyond just credit history
  • Checks crop health from satellite imagery to judge risk
  • Runs the programmable Digital Rupee, so each loan is spent only as approved
  • Watches for fraud and tracks how the loan book performs

The regulated co-operative banks stay in charge, and every credit decision is theirs.
